PubPlace

A better way to Publish

Totality Content Server allows you to publish all your content to the web including Adobe PDF, Microsoft Word, paper and high resolution images. Websites and web applications can now create, view, edit, print, scan and collaborate using content that already exists in your organization. Even better, your content can now integrate with the web-standard HTML content and functionality currently used on your websites and web applications.
